# Prompt Design

The prototype uses three persona prompts:

## 1. Direct Headhunter
Sharp, commercial, concise, focused on urgency and outcomes.

## 2. Empathetic Coach
Supportive, human, candidate-conscious, relationship-led.

## 3. Trusted Advisor
Insightful, strategic, consultative, focused on long-term value.

Each prompt uses:
- the original story
- the target person/company
- one personalised sentence
- a 120-word limit
- LinkedIn-friendly formatting

This keeps the original facts consistent while adapting the tone for different recruiter communication styles.
